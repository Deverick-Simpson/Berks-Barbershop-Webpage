Mike

Tattoo Artist

Mike Klein, a Hastings native, has been in the tattoo industry since 2011. After completing his traditional 2+ year apprenticeship he has been honing his skills and has developed a true passion for his craft. Mike tattoos in all styles and has a particular love for black and gray or "trashy polka" style tattooing. He also enjoys painting in his free time.

Mike grew up in Hastings, NE and played baseball his whole life. He eventually attended Hastings College where he also played ball before moving to Lincoln in 2000. He worked with young adults that have special needs for nearly 15 years while starting his apprenticeship and enjoyed his time helping those who needed it.

Mike is happily married with two children, a son and daughter, and is very close with his family. He is involved in his church and really enjoys giving back to his community. He looks forward to a lifelong career in tattooing and doing what he loves. Getting to help people express themselves through my art and getting to work with new people that have new stories all the time is why I love what I do and I never have to work a day in my life. - Mike
