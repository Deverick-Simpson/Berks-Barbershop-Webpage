About Us

If you didn't know

Who We Are?

Owners are brothers; Cory and Nate Berks. Matt Thornton joined November 2013. Tony Parker joined May 2015.

What We Do?

Berks Barbershop offers top notch services to the average man at affordable prices.

Why We Do It?

Berks Barbershop was established with the intention of being Lincoln's premiere barbershop. The goal was to provide our clients with the highest quality service through professional consultation, attention to detail, customer service, and consistency.

Since When?

Berks barber shop opened its doors on May 11, 2013. Visit for our anniversary Barbecue and Husker Tailgate party.

What people say about us

"I have been a regular customer of Berks Barbershop since they opened. They are doing everything right. Great haircults, priced right, relaxed atmosphere, and I really like all the barbers. They are just cool guys. It's one of Lincoln's secrets but I think the word is pretty much out now."

"You guys have the most professional shop in town, that caters to all textures of hair. Pound for pound the sharpest shop in town"

Olympic Gold Medalist Jordan Burroughs

"I have been a client of Mr.Berks for 6 years. It may seem hard to believe but I remember my first haircut from Cory. It was April 17th 2009. It was a Friday. I remember because it was an important day for me. I was receiving an award from my job at the time. As I was waiting in line for my cut, I noticed this new barber doing a design on a guy. I observed many things that impressed me. His composure, concentration, and professionalism. He wasn't loud, he didn't stop cutting to tell a story, and the end product had more detail and expression than any other I had seen before. I'm not knocking any other barbers but I saw something I felt was better than the rest. That day I only got a design from him, which everyone at the conference kept asking where I got my haircut. Mom has told me many times that "Clothes don't make the man, they make the man look better. Although a haircut makes a man feel better. I have personal witnessed many transformations under his blade. No matter color, which there are many, I've seen guys sit down in his chair and transform into Men as they stand up. ".

Marshall Currin
