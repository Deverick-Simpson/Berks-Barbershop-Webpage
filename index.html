Cory Berks

X

Cory Berks

Cory Berks, better known around Lincoln, Nebraska as Big Berks the Barber, has been cutting hair since he was 12 years old. Cory specializes in hair, art, designs, fades, crispy lines and tapers. In high school, he would charge his friend five dollars for a haircut and after graduation he spent sixteen months pursuing his barbering license at The College of Hair Design. After 5 years of working at Oasis Barber Shop, Cory’s brother Nate decided to follow in his footstep and also became a barber. The Dynamic Duo decided to open up a shop together. They named it “Berks Barber Shop” and it has been open since May of 2013.

“It’s been very rewarding opening my own shop,” Cory said. “I really enjoy it and seeing it progress. Watching it grow from nothing has been such a blessing. It’s probably been the best move I’ve ever made, opening my own shop with my brother.”

Big Berks has made a great name for himself and his family by providing the community with a modern Barbershop that is very friendly and inviting. This shop truly has something to offer everyone that walks thru the door. Berks Barber Shop is known for high quality haircuts and top notch services. Clients come from all over to sit in Cory's chair to get special designs cut into their hair. "Drawing and Barbering comes natural to me," Cory said, "so mixing the two for me is the perfect outlet for me to express my art."

Cody Hall

I started cutting hair my freshman year of high school when I moved to Omaha from Kansas City. I started cutting my cousins hair and fell in love with it. Since my cousin was my only client at the time I wanted more practice so I started cutting my own hair.

After I mastered my own fade and started making a few friends, I started choppin them up too. I grew a really strong passion for cutting hair and everything to do with barbering, so after graduating high school I went straight into barber school.

The only barber school in Nebraska was here in Lincoln so I commuted back and fourth from Omaha. During school, barbershops started seeing my work on social media and started offering me chairs. I finished school in 14 months and started at Berks Barbershop a month later feeling like it was the best spot for me. Specializing in fades and tapers come sit in my chair and get crispy. #BBS

Nate Berks

Through a business proposition and even more brotherly persuasion, I'm the second brother that contributed to molding Berks Barber Shop into what it is today. I established a passion for cutting hair through watching veterans when I was younger and practicing my own craft on fellow classmates when in high school. I moved forward in barbering to College of Hair Design where I honed my craft to become the barber I am today. After collaborating my new style with my older brothers already established clientele Berks Barber Shop was born. I specialize in fades and freestyle designs.

My favorite thing to accomplish day in and day out is a way of transformation through my clients hair and personal appearance. Taking a large amount of hair off or completely changing someone's appearance around to give them the haircut that they've always been searching for is what's important and fun to me. Making someone feel good on the inside after leaving Berks Barber Shop is what makes barbering and amazing trait for me. My attention to detail and customer appreciation keeps my clients returning with family and friends as well.

About Us

If you didn't know

Who We Are?

Owners are brothers; Cory and Nate Berks. Matt Thornton joined November 2013. Tony Parker joined May 2015.

What We Do?

Berks Barbershop offers top notch services to the average man at affordable prices.

Why We Do It?

Berks Barbershop was established with the intention of being Lincoln's premiere barbershop. The goal was to provide our clients with the highest quality service through professional consultation, attention to detail, customer service, and consistency.

Since When?

Berks barber shop opened its doors on May 11, 2013. Visit for our anniversary Barbecue and Husker Tailgate party.
