What people say about us

"I have been a client of Mr.Berks for 6 years. It may seem hard to believe but I remember my first haircut from Cory. It was April 17th 2009. It was a Friday. I remember because it was an important day for me. I was receiving an award from my job at the time. As I was waiting in line for my cut, I noticed this new barber doing a design on a guy. I observed many things that impressed me. His composure, concentration, and professionalism. He wasn't loud, he didn't stop cutting to tell a story, and the end product had more detail and expression than any other I had seen bed. I'm not knocking any other barbers but I saw something I felt was better than the rest. That day I only got a design from him, which everyone at the conference kept asking where I got my haircut. Mom has told me many times that "Clothes don't make the man, they make the man look better. Although a haircut makes a man feel better. I have personal witnessed many transformations under his blade. No matter color, which there are many, I've seen guys sit down in his chair and transform into Men as they stand up."

Marshall Currin
